Why create a 5 slide pitch deck?
In the early stages of your startup, you need to tell lots of people about what you are working on so they can help you make progress faster. Creating a 5-slide pitch deck that you can share with people will help you connect with more people and have better conversations, faster. This increases the odds that you will make important connections and ultimately succeed.
